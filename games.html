Cosmism

This is a space adventure game that me and fellow students made for our final project at NAIT.

  • Upgradeable and destroyable planets
  • Enemy squads will roam around trying to take over the universe
  • Explore the galaxy while maintaining your planets
  • Try to capture as many planets as you can

Currently the game is still in development, so here is a quick glimpse at some of the planet destruction. The full game will be posted at the end of the Semester.
Procedural Generation Demo

This is Demo that me and a research group came up with to explore topics not covered in the curiculum.

  • Endless runner where the next room is created once in the middle of the current one
  • Rooms can be built in any direction
  • Chooses between three random rooms
  • **Only a Demo** The hallway can turn into itself as it is not fully flushed out.
